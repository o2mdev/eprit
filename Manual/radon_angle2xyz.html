radon_angle2xyz

Conversion of projection angles into unit vector projections

Syntax:

radon_pars = radon_angle2xyz(radon_pars)

Description:

radon_pars - structure of parameters ~.Theta, ~.Phi - projection angles radon_pars - structure of parameters ~.un - unit vector projections on coordinate axis

Example:

% Define parameters radon_pars.Theta=50; radon_pars.Phi=30; radon_pars=radon_angle2xyz(radon_pars)
