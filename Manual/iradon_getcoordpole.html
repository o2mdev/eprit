iradon_GetCoordPole

Return numerical and string representation of the coordinate pole used for imaging.

Syntax:

[ipole] = iradon_GetCoordPole(pole) [ipole, cpole] = iradon_GetCoordPole(pole)

Description:

Returns numerical and string representation of the coordinate pole used for imaging. The angles Θ and φ are given relative to this pole (Z in the figure below). pole - coordinate pole (int, 1/2/3 or string, 'X'/'Y'/'Z') ipole - coordinate pole (int, 1/2/3) cpole - coordinate pole (string, 'X'/'Y'/'Z')
